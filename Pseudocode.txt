if mark is greater or equal 80 and less or equal 100
    Grade is High Distinction
else if mark is greater or equal 70 and less or equal 79

else if mark is greater or equal 60 and less or equal 69
    Grade is Credit
else if mark is greater or equal 50 and less or equal 59
    Grade is Pass
elif if mark is greater or equal 0 and less or equal 49
    Grade is Fail
return the value of grade
